QXKJ-1007 CAT6A / CAT6 / CAT5E UTP Toolless Keystone Jack

QXKJ-1007

Application

The QXKJ-1007 UTP toolless keystone jack is designed for structured cabling outlets, unloaded patch panels and modular wall plate applications where RJ45 copper connectivity is required. The family supports CAT6A, CAT6 and CAT5E model selection for project installation, distributor inventory and private-label sourcing.

Specification

The jack shall be supplied as a UTP toolless RJ45 keystone module with source-listed CAT6A, CAT6 and CAT5E grade options. Termination shall use a toolless IDC structure. Source-imported electrical values include 1.5 Amps current rating, insulation resistance of at least 500 MOhm, contact resistance of no more than 10 mOhm and DC resistance of no more than 0.1 Ohm.

Physical construction shall use high-impact flame-retardant plastic listed as UL 94V-0 rated in source text, FR-4 PCB at 1.2 mm thickness, phosphor bronze jack wire with gold over nickel plating and tin-plated phosphor bronze IDC conductor. Certificate files and final market approvals remain confirmation needed before public claim use.

Features & Benefits

  • Toolless IDC termination for installation without a punch-down tool.
  • One product family covers CAT6A, CAT6 and CAT5E purchasing choices.
  • Accommodates 26 to 23 AWG solid conductors.
  • Plug insertion life source-listed at at least 750 cycles with FCC compliant RJ45 plug.
  • Plug retention force source-listed at at least 11 lbf.
  • FR-4 PCB and phosphor bronze contact construction are listed in the imported source data.
  • Private-label requirements can be quoted by grade, logo, carton mark and package plan.
