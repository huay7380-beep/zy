# Standard Product Onboarding Checklist

Use this checklist every time a new structured cabling product or product family is added.

## 1. Source Intake

- [ ] Save original supplier catalogue, quote sheet, photos, videos, certificates and packaging files.
- [ ] Record source file path, page number, visible factory brand, factory model and extraction confidence.
- [ ] Decide whether the product is unchanged OEM/private-label, modified OEM, or new development.
- [ ] Mark the record as `source_received`.

## 2. Product Master

- [ ] Assign `family_id`.
- [ ] Create internal `public_sku` and map it to the factory model.
- [ ] Fill grade, shielding, port count, rack size, material, color, termination, cable/jacket, and PDU electrical fields where applicable.
- [ ] Record MOQ, sample availability, lead time, packaging and factory cost.
- [ ] Mark missing fields as blockers instead of guessing.

## 3. Brand Replacement

- [ ] Confirm public brand name, logo, domain, email signature and catalogue style.
- [ ] Remove or hide old brand from public site copy, image watermarks, catalogue pages and file names.
- [ ] Keep factory model and supplier identity in internal audit fields.
- [ ] Confirm private-label authorization or OEM/ODM cooperation evidence.

## 4. Compliance Gate

- [ ] Assign compliance profile: passive network component, rack accessory, cable/patch cord, telecom accessory, or PDU power product.
- [ ] Collect RoHS/REACH/material declarations where needed.
- [ ] For EU installation cables, check whether CPR/fire classification applies.
- [ ] For PDU/electrical products, require country plug type, rating label, test report and professional review.
- [ ] Do not publish CE/UL/ETL/FCC or other certification claims without matching documents.

## 5. Content Package

- [ ] White background main image.
- [ ] Detail images: front, back, side, terminal/IDC/detail, material detail.
- [ ] Application images: rack, telecom room, office wall plate, data center, project installation.
- [ ] Packaging image and carton label.
- [ ] English title, short description, technical specification, FAQ and datasheet.

## 6. Commercial Package

- [ ] Price tiers by quantity.
- [ ] Sample price and freight rule.
- [ ] MOQ and mixed-SKU carton policy.
- [ ] Incoterms assumptions.
- [ ] Quote validity period.
- [ ] Margin floor and approval gate.

## 7. Site And RFQ

- [ ] Publish only after content, compliance and price review.
- [ ] Add to category page and related-product bundles.
- [ ] Confirm RFQ fields are enough for quote: grade, shielding, ports, length, color, plug type, target country, quantity.
- [ ] Add download CTA for catalogue/spec sheet.

## 8. Live Operations

- [ ] Monitor product views, RFQ submits, downloads, keyword ranking and quote win/loss.
- [ ] Update product FAQ from real inquiries.
- [ ] Archive quote drafts, approvals, customer objections and follow-up results.
- [ ] Review stale products monthly and retire or refresh weak SKUs.
